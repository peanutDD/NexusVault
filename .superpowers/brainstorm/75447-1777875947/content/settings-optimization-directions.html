Larger settings hub

Reorganize settings more deeply and introduce new sections. Most useful long-term, but highest risk for a “do not break function” request.

Recommendation

I recommend A plus a few safe pieces of B: polish the current page, add active/accessible navigation only if it can be covered by tests, and avoid new backend-dependent features.
